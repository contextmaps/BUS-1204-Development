Learning Objectives

By the end of this agent segment, students will be able to:

  1. Run a domain task end-to-end through their deployed Protocol v3 custom agent.

  4. Identify the single most important v3 observation from the lab — what v3 did that v2 would not have.

S12.2a.1 Standard Prompt AI: respond
Here is the domain task you will run through your custom agent: TASK: Help me draft a concise business recommendation memo (3 short paragraphs) for the following scenario. The memo will summarize the situation, identify the key trade-off, and give a recommendation. --- SCENARIO (from a startup strategy briefing): Meridian Coffee Roasters, a regional specialty coffee company operating 12 retail locations in the mid-Atlantic, is evaluating whether to launch a direct-to-consumer subscription service. The company has strong brand recognition among local coffee enthusiasts but has no e-commerce infrastructure. The subscription model would require upfront investment in logistics software, packaging, and fulfillment operations estimated at $180,000. Their marketing director, Priya Mehta, argues the subscription model would extend brand reach beyond their regional footprint and create predictable monthly revenue. Their operations director argues that fulfillment complexity at scale could erode the premium quality perception the brand has spent eight years building. The board wants a 3-paragraph recommendation memo: paragraph one summarizes the situation and the core tension; paragraph two identifies the single most important decision criterion; paragraph three gives a clear recommendation with one condition attached. --- Please draft the 3-paragraph memo. My agent instructions specify how you should approach this.
